Sining Laban sa Katiwalian

“Sining Laban sa Katiwalian” is a creative movement that integrates various art forms including visual, applied, literary, and performative arts to reveal the social, cultural, political, historical, and economic realities of corruption in the Philippines.

About the Virtual Museum

Sining Laban sa Katiwalian is a virtual museum that uses art to confront and question the realities of corruption in society. Through guided art appreciation, viewers are encouraged to examine symbolism, emotion, and technique to understand how artists communicate resistance and truth. Each piece becomes a space for reflection, helping audiences see how creativity can expose injustice and inspire action. In this digital gallery, art is not just observed, it becomes a powerful tool for awareness, dialogue, and collective change.
